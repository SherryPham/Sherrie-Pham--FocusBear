# Company Reflection — Focus Bear

## 1. Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
Focus Bear was built by founder Jeremy Nagel because no existing productivity tool suitable for him as his feelings. He'd tried every blocker, app, and trick, none stuck, so he built the one he wished. 


## 2. What problem is Focus Bear solving?
Distraction, broken routines, and burnout — particularly for people with ADHD/Autism. It blocks distractions across desktop and mobile, walks users through morning habits in real time and prompts breaks to prevent hyper-focus burnout. 

## 3. Why do you think this mission is important?
ADHD or Autism diagnoses are rising, and many late-diagnosed adults have spent years feeling broken for not being able to do what "should" be simple. Tools designed for how their brains actually work make a real difference to focus, health, and self-esteem.

## 4. How does Focus Bear's work align with your personal values or interests?
I find behaviour change interesting. Focus Bear locks the apps that I usually use for entertainment until my routine is done. This help me change my usual behaviour more efficienly.

## 5. Do you personally relate to any of the challenges Focus Bear aims to solve?
Yes. The notification system is the main distracting factor. I always pick it up to check the notifications when hearing the sounds. The idea of my phone going into quiet mode automatically when I start a focus session is very useful.
